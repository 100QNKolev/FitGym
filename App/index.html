About Us

FitGym was founded in 1991 as a family owned and operated business specialising in supplying high-quality gym equipment at great prices. Instead of being just another gym equipment retailer, our founders wanted to be the best in the industry and set their minds to doing so! Over the last two decades FitGym has grown into one of Bulgaria’s largest online fitness equipment retailers, helping thousands of customers live longer, happier and healthier lives.
